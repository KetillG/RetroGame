Bomberman

Instructions

Controls:

Player 1: Moves with ASDW.

Player 2: Moves with arrow keys.

Aim of the game:

Blow up your oppnenet before they kill you. All players have three lives.

Powerups:

  • Collect bombs to be able to drop more than one bomb at a time.
  • Collect shoes to run faster.
  • Collect the fire to increase the power of your bombs.
